Who We Are

Errandss is a community-powered platform that connects people who need help with daily tasks, services, or local products — to trusted individuals nearby who can get it done.

Whether it's running errands, finding skilled help, or buying homemade goods, we make everyday life easier, faster, and more local.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower gig workers, homemakers, students, and small businesses by giving them flexible earning opportunities while helping clients save time and access quality local services.
